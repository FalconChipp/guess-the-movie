How to Play:

  • Enter your name in the input field.
  • Select a category or leave it as "All" to include movies from all categories.
  • Choose the game difficulty by clicking on the Easy or Hard button.
  • Click the Start button to begin the game.
  • Guess the movies based on the given actor's filmography. Enter your guesses in the input field.
  • If your guess matches a movie title, it will be marked as green and your score will increase.
  • If your guess is close to a movie title, it will be marked as orange, but your score will not increase.
  • If your guess is incorrect, it will be displayed as is, and your score will not increase.
  • The game ends when you guess all the movies correctly or when the time runs out.
